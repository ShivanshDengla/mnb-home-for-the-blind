Appeal for sponsorship & projects

We appeal to well-wishers to sponsor and financially support current projects: AKSHAY OORJA (sustainable energy), renovation of the Home, and Acupressure/Massage Training.

The Home serves sightless and partially sighted persons from all walks of life and any state of India, helping them become self-reliant and economically independent. Your generosity will directly improve the quality of life for those denied the gift of vision. Motto: EACH ONE REACH ONE.

Yours truly,
Dr. Vimal Kumar Dengla
Hon. Secretary
