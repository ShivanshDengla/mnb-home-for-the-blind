Programmes

Training that leads to livelihoods

Practical skills, technology, and entrepreneurial support to meet each trainee where they are.

Massage training

Foot and chair massage courses that expand employment opportunities for trainees.

Advanced computer training

MNB–TCS All-India centre grooming visually challenged learners in MS-CIT and advanced skills.

Computer centre

MS-CIT and basic computing with screen readers covering Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and internet use.

Self-employment schemes

Mini and Maxi schemes offer equipment and materials in kind so trainees can start sustainable businesses.

Subsidised hostel

Affordable accommodation for visually impaired men relocating to Mumbai while they establish careers.

Vocational training

Four-year residential programme with weaving, handloom, tailoring, cane work, and other trades.

Facilities & support

Medical & recreation

Low-vision treatment, music, chess, carrom, and home gym for holistic wellbeing.

In-kind support

Equipment, materials, and operational help instead of cash to set up sustainable livelihoods.

Career readiness

IT and vocational pathways designed to match abilities and employer expectations.
