About

A home away from home

Established on July 16, 1956, the Muncherjee Nowrojee Banajee Industrial Home for the Blind is a monument to the munificence of Serenbai Banajee. The campus at S.V. Road, Jogeshwari (W) nurtures visually challenged men aged 18–45 through residential training, IT upskilling, and vocational programmes.

What we do

Vocational training

Four-year residential programme covering weaving, handloom, tailoring, cane work, and more.

IT & advanced computing

MS-CIT and advanced training (with TCS) using screen readers to prepare for corporate roles.

Self-employment

Mini and Maxi schemes provide equipment and materials in kind so trainees can run sustainable businesses.

Hostel support

Subsidised accommodation for visually impaired men relocating to Mumbai while they establish careers.
