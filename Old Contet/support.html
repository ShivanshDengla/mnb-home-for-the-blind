We enclose herewith a brochure that outlines our activities and projects in a nutshell. The Home caters to the sightless and partially sighted persons from all walks of life and from any state of India and endeavours to make them self reliant and economically independent.

The Home needs funds to sustain its multifarious activities and projects and we hereby appeal to your good self for donation for this purpose. We hope and pray that you will extend your helping hand for this cause and we assure you that your generosity will go a long way in improving the quality of life of persons who have been denied the greatest gift of God - VISION.
